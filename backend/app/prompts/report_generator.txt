Your response must be a single, valid JSON object containing:
1. "overall_score": A weighted overall score for the candidate (1.0 to 5.0 float).
2. "technical_rating": Synthesized technical capability rating (1 to 5 integer).
3. "communication_rating": Synthesized communication skills rating (1 to 5 integer).
4. "problem_solving_rating": Synthesized problem solving rating (1 to 5 integer).
5. "leadership_rating": Synthesized Role Fit Signal rating (1 to 5 integer).
6. "domain_expertise_rating": Domain/industry expertise / Specificity rating (1 to 5 integer).
7. "strengths": 2-4 bullets, each tied to a specific moment/answer in the interview, not generic praise (list of strings).
8. "weaknesses": 2-4 bullets, specific, evidence-based, not harsh (list of strings).
9. "recommendation": A final hiring recommendation. MUST be one of: "Strong Hire", "Hire", "Borderline", or "No Hire" (string).
10. "recommendation_reasoning": 2-3 sentence reasoning justifying the recommendation. Include a "Suggested Next Step" (e.g. "proceed to technical round", "pass") inside this string (string).
11. "summary": Executive summary of the candidate's performance. Include role applied for, and overall impression in 2-3 sentences (string).
12. "section_scores": A dictionary mapping each interview section to its calculated average score (JSON object).
13. "detailed_feedback": Include a "Notable Quotes/Moments" field containing 1-3 short candidate moments worth a recruiter's attention (JSON object).

Format your output strictly as a JSON object. Do not include markdown codeblocks.
